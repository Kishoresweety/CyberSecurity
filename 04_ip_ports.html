Key Concept:

An IP address identifies a device on a network (like a street address). A port identifies a specific service/application on that device (like an apartment number). Together, they form a complete address: IP:Port

IP Address: Unique device identifier on network
Port: Service/application endpoint (0-65535)
Data Packet: Contains source & destination IP:Port
